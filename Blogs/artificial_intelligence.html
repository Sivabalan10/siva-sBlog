What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence refers to programs that can think, reason, and solve problems. It's like teaching computers to be smart and make decisions.

Types of AI:

  • Narrow AI: This type of AI is specialized for specific tasks, like playing chess or recognizing faces.
  • General AI: Imagine a super-smart AI that can handle any task a human can. That's the idea behind General AI, but we're not there yet.
  • Super AI: This is an AI that's smarter than humans in every way. It's a bit like what you see in sci-fi movies, where AI surpasses human intelligence.
Understanding AI types helps us see how smart these programs can be. AI is like having a really clever friend who can learn and solve problems, and it's shaping the future in amazing ways!
